## The Ask

We need a concise, well-reasoned answer to the following question:

> **What are the three most important things to consider when starting a new software project?**

This is for a general technical leadership audience — people who have started projects before but want a clear framework for what to prioritize upfront.

## Deliverable

A polished one-page memo that a technical leader could share with their team at project kickoff. Not a listicle — a readable, authoritative piece.

## Process

1. Assign research to example-researcher
2. Review findings and assign writing to example-writer
3. Review memo and confirm mission complete

## Success Criteria

- Three clearly identified considerations with solid rationale
- Memo is readable and free of jargon
- Suitable for sharing without editing
