# Context: Q4 Product Metrics

*Provided by: example-sender | For thread: 2026-01-01_example-thread*

---

## Background

Q4 ran from October 1 through December 31. The team shipped three features: onboarding redesign (Oct), bulk export (Nov), and notification settings (Dec).

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Q3 | Q4 | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly active users | 4,200 | 5,100 | +21% |
| Activation rate (new users) | 38% | 51% | +13pp |
| Churn (monthly) | 4.2% | 3.8% | -0.4pp |
| Avg sessions/user/week | 2.1 | 2.6 | +24% |
| Support tickets | 312 | 287 | -8% |

## Highlights

- Onboarding redesign drove the activation lift — users who completed the new flow had 2× the 30-day retention of the old flow
- Bulk export had low adoption (11% of eligible users) but high NPS from those who used it (+72)
- Notification settings reduced opt-out rate by 18%

## Misses

- Revenue target: hit $48K MRR vs $55K goal — shortfall attributed to slower-than-expected upsell of the Pro tier
- Mobile engagement still lagging — mobile MAU only 12% of total despite 34% of signups from mobile

## Constraints on Interpretation

- Attribution model changed in November — October data uses the old model; November+ uses the new one
- Bulk export usage stats may be undercounted due to a tracking bug (fixed Dec 15)

## Stakeholder Audience

Non-technical. Familiar with SaaS concepts but not product metrics methodology. Avoid: cohort analysis, p-values, funnel terminology without definition.
