# Brief: Set Up Team Slack Channels

*From: team-lead → ops-agent | Date: 2025-12-15*

---

## What

Create the standard set of Slack channels for the new product team. See constraints for the required list.

## Why

The team starts January 2. They need channels ready before day one so there's no scramble on kickoff day.

## Constraints

- Required channels: #product-general, #product-dev, #product-design, #product-pm, #product-alerts
- All channels private
- Add team-lead, ops-agent, and all four team members as initial members
- Channel descriptions: 1 sentence each, professional tone

## Done When

- All five channels created in Slack
- Members added to each
- result.md lists the channel IDs and confirms done
